A good workout plan helps you stay consistent, avoid injury, and achieve better results over time.

At Fitness Club, we recommend balancing cardio, strength, mobility, and recovery during the week.

Rest is an essential part of progress because muscles need time to recover and become stronger.

Weekly Exercise Routine

Day Exercise Duration
Monday Cardio Training 30 minutes
Tuesday Strength Training 45 minutes
Wednesday Recovery and Stretching 20 minutes
Thursday Yoga and Mobility 40 minutes
Friday Cardio Intervals 30 minutes
Saturday Full Body Strength 45 minutes
Sunday Rest -

This schedule is a general example. Beginners can start with shorter sessions, while advanced members can increase intensity based on their goals.

For best results, combine training with enough sleep, hydration, and balanced nutrition.
